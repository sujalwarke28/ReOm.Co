# ReOm.Co Demonstration Guide

This guide provides a step-by-step walkthrough for demonstrating the ReOm.Co platform across all role-based portals.

## 1. The Admin Portal

**Persona:** System Administrator
**Goal:** Manage users, oversee platform health, and resolve bottlenecks.

**Step-by-Step:**
1. **Login:** Sign in using an Admin account.
2. **Dashboard Overview:** Observe the comprehensive KPI grid. Highlight that Admins see all tasks and approvals across the entire organization.
3. **User Management:** Navigate to the "Users" tab.
   - Demonstrate the ability to approve or reject pending user registrations.
   - Show how to change a user's role (e.g., promoting a Manager to Executive).
4. **Approval Override:** Navigate to the "Approvals" tab. Point out that Admins have the universal ability to approve or reject any pending request, regardless of who it was assigned to.

## 2. The Executive Portal

**Persona:** C-Level Executive or Director
**Goal:** Monitor organizational performance and high-level KPIs without getting bogged down in daily operations.

**Step-by-Step:**
1. **Login:** Sign in using an Executive account.
2. **Executive Dashboard:** Notice the dedicated Executive Dashboard greeting on the home page.
3. **Executive Portal Tab:** Navigate to the "Executive Portal".
   - Showcase the "CONFIDENTIAL" badges.
   - Demonstrate the high-level financial and operational metrics (e.g., Operational Costs, Compliance Rate).
   - Show the interactive charts depicting task completion rates over time.

## 3. The Manager Portal

**Persona:** Department Manager
**Goal:** Assign tasks to operational staff and review approval requests from their team.

**Step-by-Step:**
1. **Login:** Sign in using a Manager account.
2. **Task Assignment:** Navigate to the "Tasks" tab.
   - Click "New Task".
   - Fill out the task details and assign it to an Operational Staff member.
3. **Reviewing Approvals:** Navigate to the "Approvals" tab.
   - Locate an approval request submitted by a staff member.
   - Review the details and click "Approve" or "Reject".

## 4. The Operational Staff Portal

**Persona:** Floor Staff, Cashier, or Warehouse Worker
**Goal:** Complete assigned tasks and request permissions for exceptions.

**Step-by-Step:**
1. **Login:** Sign in using an Operational Staff account (like your `OpsSujal` account).
2. **Task Execution:** Navigate to the "Tasks" tab.
   - View the list of tasks specifically assigned to this user.
   - Click the "Complete" button on a pending task. 
3. **Submitting Approvals:** Navigate to the "Approvals" tab.
   - Click "Submit Request" (e.g., "Requesting a Leave on Tuesday").
   - Open the "Request Approval From" dropdown (now fully populated).
   - Select a Manager or Executive and submit the request.
